About the Project

The Turbulence Duality Open Project is a non-profit initiative to aggregate, verify, and popularize revolutionary scientific theories. Starting with turbulence theory and its duality with string theory, the project aims to create an open platform powered by AI to inspire and accelerate scientific discovery.

Features

  • AI Tools: Use advanced AI to validate theories and connect concepts across disciplines.
  • Collaborative Platform: Enable researchers to contribute, critique, and interact with data and theory.
  • Expanding Horizons: Begin with turbulence and expand to domains like astronomy, biology, and quantum physics.
