Overview

This page reviews recent work on velocity circulation statistics in hydrodynamic turbulence, with connections to the Loop Equation and Area Law in Turbulence (Migdal, 1995).

Key Research Works

  • Alexander Migdal: "Loop Equation and Area Law in Turbulence" [Link]
  • Kartik P. Iyer, Katepalli R. Sreenivasan, P. K. Yeung: "Circulation in High Reynolds Number Isotropic Turbulence is a Bifractal" [Link]
  • Alexander Migdal: "Statistical Equilibrium of Circulating Fluids" [Link]
  • Gabriel Apolinario, Luca Moriconi, Rodrigo Pereira, Victor Valadão: "Vortex Gas Modeling of Turbulent Circulation Statistics" [Link]
