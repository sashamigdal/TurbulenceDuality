Turbulence Duality

Reveal the Beauty of Primes Under the Ugly Mask of Chaos.

The turbulence problem has stood unsolved for centuries for a good reason — a direct approach is virtually impossible, as the vorticity in a turbulent fluid does not exist as a smooth vector field. The breakthrough described here introduces a new concept of turbulence duality, reducing strong fluctuations of the classical vector field in 3D to a weakly fluctuating dual quantum problem in one dimension.
