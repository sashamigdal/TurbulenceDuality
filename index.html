Turbulence Duality

Reveal the Beauty of Primes Under the Ugly Mask of Chaos.

The turbulence problem has stood unsolved for centuries for a good reason — a direct approach is virtually impossible, as the vorticity in a turbulent fluid does not exist as a smooth vector field. The breakthrough described here introduces a new concept of duality, reducing strong fluctuations of the vector field to a weakly fluctuating dual problem in another dimension.

Duality of Turbulence and String Theory

A presentation from the 127th Statistical Mechanics Conference (December 2024).

Turbulence, a cornerstone of fluid mechanics, has a surprising dual: string theory on regular star polygons. This groundbreaking work connects chaotic vortex motion to elegant geometric structures.
